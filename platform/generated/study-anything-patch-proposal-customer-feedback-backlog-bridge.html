# Patch Proposal Customer Feedback Backlog Bridge

Metadata-only proof that accepted customer-feedback intake receipts can emit Product Loop backlog signals without assigning priority or triggering follow-up/execution.

- status: `pass`
- schema: `patch-proposal-customer-feedback-backlog-bridge-v1`
- queued backlog signals: `3`
- blocked backlog signals: `10`

## Claim Boundary

An accepted patch-proposal customer-feedback intake receipt can become a metadata-only Product Loop backlog signal. The signal is not prioritized, not sent to a customer, not executed, and not promoted to production.

Not claimed:
- raw customer reply included
- private customer data included
- automatic priority assigned
- Study Anything sent a follow-up
- Study Anything changed source
- Study Anything changed production
- model call performed
- customer satisfaction certified
