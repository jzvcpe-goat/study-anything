## Claim Boundary

The envelope proves only metadata-only customer delivery review preparation. It does not send, publish, deploy, comment on PRs, or certify customer-visible content.

Not claimed:
- customer-visible draft body included
- customer message sent
- PR comment created
- external publication approved
- production change approved
- truth or security certified
