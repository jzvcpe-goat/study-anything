# Commercial Model

Study Anything follows an Obsidian-inspired trust model while remaining Apache-2.0 open source.

## Free Core

The core app remains free and self-hostable:

- Full local learning workflow.
- Local data ownership.
- Community plugins.
- Bring Your Own Agent providers.
- No account required.

## Paid Convenience Services

Paid services should sell convenience, reliability, collaboration, and hosting, not access to the core workflow.

- Study Sync: encrypted backup/sync.
- Study Publish: publish selected learning artifacts.
- Study Teams: collaboration and admin controls.
- Catalyst: optional supporter tier.

## PMF Before Monetization

No hosted billing is part of the MVP. The product should first prove repeated learning value, community trust, extension demand, and self-host adoption.
